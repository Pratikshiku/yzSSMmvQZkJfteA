## Second-hand Car Trading System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/190933ed09de49b19d5925dc566d5345/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User, Merchant, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Cars
5. Forum
6. Announcement Information
7. Personal Center
8. Car Favorites
9. Change Password
10. Car Information
11. Car Comments
12. Car Favorites
13. Forum Management
14. Announcement Information Management

**Merchant Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Car Management
6. Car Comments Management
7. Car Favorites Management
8. Forum Management
9. Announcement Information Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Administrator Management
4. Car Management
5. Car Comments Management
6. Car Favorites Management
7. Car Brand Management
8. Announcement Type Management
9. Forum Management
10. Announcement Information Management
11. Merchant Management
12. User Management
13. Carousel Information
